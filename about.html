Ilham Safeek

Founder & Chief Architect

Ilham Safeek is a seasoned Software Architect, entrepreneur, and educator with a strong track record in delivering enterprise-grade software solutions. As the Founder and Chief Architect of Hashnate Software Engineering, Ilham has led the development of scalable systems in ERP, CRM, and IoT, serving clients in Sri Lanka, the Middle East, Europe, and the United States.

Expanding his vision beyond borders, Ilham co-founded Informake Software Solutions in Oman, where he plays a strategic role in driving digital adoption for businesses through customized software, sales consulting, and ERP implementations.
